Our Story

A Dream Born
in the Himalayas

"We didn't start a business. We started a movement — to make the mountains accessible, safe, and magical for every Indian."

Tour & Travel started in 2020 when our founder Arjun Rawat, a passionate mountaineer from Rishikesh, noticed that most travel companies treated the mountains as a backdrop — not a destination with a soul. He set out to change that.

What began as a small group of 8 friends trekking to Kedarnath has grown into one of India's most trusted travel companies — serving over 500 travellers every year across Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan, and beyond.

Our Core

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every decision, every route, and every experience we create.

Our Mission

To make extraordinary travel accessible to every Indian by combining local expertise, complete safety, and deeply personal service. We believe a great journey is not about where you go — it's about how you feel when you return.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved travel company — one where every traveller feels cared for like family. We envision a world where sustainable tourism empowers local communities and protects the landscapes we love.

Our Promise

Every tour is a personal promise from us to you. We promise safety, authenticity, fair pricing, and experiences that will stay with you long after you return home. We keep our promises — always.

Safety Above All

Every trek and tour is led by certified guides with full safety equipment, first-aid kits, and emergency protocols. Your safety is never compromised.

Community First

We hire local guides, partner with local homestays, and reinvest in the communities that make our journeys possible. Travel should uplift, not exploit.

Eco-Responsibility

All our tours follow Leave No Trace principles. We carry out what we carry in, ban single-use plastics, and actively participate in trail clean-up drives.

Authentic Experiences

No cookie-cutter itineraries. We create journeys that connect you with real India — its people, food, traditions, and untouched natural beauty.

Traveller-Centric Care

From your first enquiry to your safe return, our team is available 24/7. You are never alone on this journey — we walk every step with you.

Honest Pricing

No hidden charges, no last-minute surprises. What we quote is what you pay. We believe in complete transparency because trust is our most valuable currency.
